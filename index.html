Wild Quest is 2 player quiz game for kids.


As you press contiune, 9 blocks will be displayed.


Click start to enter Player names. Player 1 gets first turn


Click on any one of 9 boxs in the middel. A hint & fact about the animal is displayed.


Type in your guess in textbox and press submit.


Player gets a point for correct answer. Take turns.


Any player reaching 5 points, get to unlock Win Quest & correct guess, wins the game.
