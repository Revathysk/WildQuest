Wild Quest is 2 player quiz game.


Press start button, nine box will be displayed.


Player 1 will get chance to click on one of the box.


A hint about the animal and fact will popup.


Type in your guess in textbox and press submit.


In case of correct answer player gets a point. Take turns.


Any player reaching 5 points gets to unlock Win Quest & correct guess, wins the game.
